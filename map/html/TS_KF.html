INSTRUCTION A - Do not apply the pesticide within 200 feet if applied by ground nor within 400 feet if applied by air, of fresh-water ponds (including natural or manmade, such as stock ponds), slow moving streams or pools within streams, vernal pools and other ephemeral or permanent water bodies which typically support inundation during winter rains and hold water for a minimum of 12 weeks in a year of average rainfall.

INSTRUCTION B - Do not apply the pesticide within 600 feet of all denning areas, defined as a circle 100 feet in radius, around the set of holes in the ground forming the surface of the den, as depicted in the California Department of Pesticide Regulation’s Endangered Species Project, San Joaquin kit fox denning characteristics fact card. http://www.cdpr.ca.gov/docs/endspec/espdfs/sjkfden.pdf
